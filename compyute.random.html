compyute.random

Randomness based tensor functions.

compyute.random.normal(shape, mean=0.0, std=1.0, dtype=None, device=Dtype('cpu'))[source]

Creates a tensor with values drawn from a normal distribution.

Parameters:

  • shape (_ShapeLike) – Shape of the new tensor.

  • mean (float, optional) – Mean of random values. Defaults to 0.

  • std (float, optional) – Standard deviation of random values. Defaults to 1.

  • dtype (_DtypeLike, optional) – Datatype of the tensor data. Defaults to None.

  • device (_DeviceLike, optional) – The device the tensor is stored on. Defaults to compyute.cpu.

Returns:

Tensor of normally distributed samples.

Return type:

Tensor
